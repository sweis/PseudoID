You can use this private identity provider to anonymously log into any website that supports OpenID! Just follow these simple steps.

  1. Create a nickname or pseudonym at the Tiresias Blind Signer.
  2. Visit any OpenID-enabled website and enter {{ request_url_without_path }}/server as your OpenID. Try these:
    • Puffypoodles: Google Step2 Demo
    • JanRain Consumer: Python OpenID Example Consumer
    • Plaxo: Social Networking Site
  3. You're Done! You'll be logged in under your nickname.
How It Works

The Tiresias private identity provider authenticates users anonymously by piggbyacking on the Tiresias blind signer. When you create a nickname at the blind signer, you are asked to login normally using your google account. Once the blind signer knows that you are a valid user, it issues you an access token with your chosen nickname. The token is blindly signed by the signer such that Tiresias will later recognize it as being created by a valid user, but will not be able to tell which user created the token.
